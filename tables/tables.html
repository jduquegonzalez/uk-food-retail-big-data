Section 4.3.2.1: Metric Definitions and Formulas

Visual methodology for calculating Surplus % of Food Handled ‚Äî the key metric for cross-retailer comparison.

üí° Why Surplus % Handled?

Waste = End-state metric
Measures food discarded (consequence)
Surplus = Root cause metric
Measures forecasting inaccuracy (cause)

To evaluate Big Data's impact on inventory efficiency, we analyse the cause, not just the consequence.

1 Food Handled (tonnes)

Total volume flowing through the retailer's operation. Derived using the mass-balance principle.

Food Handled = Sold + Waste + Redistribution

‚ìò Most retailers do not explicitly disclose this figure. Calculated where inputs must equal outputs.

2 Total Surplus (tonnes)

Total overstock regardless of outcome. This is the root cause metric for forecasting accuracy.

Total Surplus = Waste + Redistribution
